--- layout: default title: Doubt journal --- Welcome.

Our goal is to highlight exceptional academic contributions. We review candidate contributions and apply doubt.

Reviews will endeavour to explain why a given paper is or is not important.

Frequently asked questions
Q: How can I submit a paper? A: You cannot.
Q: How can I contest a negative review? A: You cannot.
Q: What happens if my paper is accepted? A: Nothing.
Q: How can I support this journal? A: Stop writing vacuous papers.


Extraordinary claims require extraordinary evidence.
